✦ Featured Project

Research · Final Year Project

DeepGuard · AV Deepfake Detection

A browser extension that passively detects deepfakes in live video calls and KYC portals, no user action needed. Built on EfficientNet-B4 and Wav2Vec 2.0 with a cross-attention fusion layer and temporal LSTM. Trained on FakeAVCeleb, tested on LAV-DF for true cross-dataset generalization. Targeting a research paper submission.
